## Notable findings

- `betfairlightweight` + `flumine` remain the de facto Betfair Python stack (480★ / 229★, both actively maintained).
- Kalshi has a sophisticated bot ecosystem already — OctagonAI's 5-gate risk engine and ryanfrigo's multi-agent setup are the closest analogues to our vision.
- Paper from Clinton & Huang shows **persistent arbitrage windows** across Kalshi vs Polymarket on the 2024 election — structural settlement differences create opportunities but settlement semantics must be modelled.
- Hetzner Frankfurt + QuantVPS Chicago is the honest £30–50/mo stack. AWS only makes sense for backtest bursts and S3 historical data.
- CLV is the single most important metric to track from day one. Half-Kelly is industry standard.
- Karpathy's LLM-wiki pattern maps directly onto our research flywheel: wiki is both the agent's notebook and its memory; research compounds instead of resetting.
